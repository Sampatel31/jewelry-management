# Privacy Policy

**Shrigar Jewellers Management System**
Last Updated: 2024

---

## 1. Data We Collect

The Shrigar Jewellers Management System collects only the business data that you manually enter into the application:

- Shop / store information (name, address, contact details)
- Product and inventory records
- Customer records and purchase history
- Billing and invoice data
- Supplier and purchase order records
- Employee / user accounts (name, email, role)
- Gold rate and metal price entries

**We collect NO telemetry, usage analytics, crash reports, or any data automatically.** No data is ever sent to any external server, cloud, or third party.

---

## 2. Data Storage

All data entered into this application is stored **exclusively on your local computer** in a PostgreSQL database running locally on your machine.

- Data location: your computer's hard drive
- Database port: `5433` (local, not accessible over the internet by default)
- Backups: saved to `~/ShrigarJewellers/backups/` on your computer

The application requires **no internet connection** after initial installation.

---

## 3. Data Ownership

**YOU own all your data.** We (the developer) have **zero access** to your business information. There is no "account" with us, no login to our servers, and no data ever leaves your computer.

Your data includes:
- All customer records
- All billing and invoice history
- All inventory and product data
- All financial records

---

## 4. No Internet Required

This application is designed to work **100% offline**. After installation:

- No internet connection is required for any feature
- No data is synced to any cloud service
- No background network requests are made
- You can use the application without any network access permanently

---

## 5. Backups

The application automatically creates daily backups at 11 PM and saves them to `~/ShrigarJewellers/backups/`. You have full control over these backup files:

- You can copy them to a USB drive, external hard disk, or any location
- You can delete them at any time
- The application retains the last 30 backups automatically
- On uninstall, your backup files remain in `~/ShrigarJewellers/` â€” they are NOT deleted
